Data Deletion Request

To request the deletion of your account data, please follow these steps:

  1. Login to your Facebook account.
  2. Navigate to your account settings.
  3. Find the connected apps and games section.
  4. Locate "Sky Puck Showdown Powered-Up" in the list.
  5. Remove the game from your Facebook account.
  6. If logged in, log out from the game to ensure the data is no longer accessed.

Please note that when you use Facebook login, we only collect and store your name or gaming profile name, and your profile picture or gaming profile picture.

Types of Data Kept:
- Name or gaming profile name
- Profile picture or gaming profile picture

Data Deletion:
We don't delete any of your data permanentaly since there are no sensitive data collected, also you will of course be able to retreive it if you tried to log in again with same facebook account.
